About Me <3

Hi, I'm Jessica!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. If you ever want to chat, you can find me on Discord at jessicathepixie.

I've always had a passion for pretty things. Ever since I was young, I've been drawn to dresses, skirts, and the idea of being a cheerleader. Basically, I've always wanted to be a girl. Unfortunately, I come from a family that wouldn't have supported this part of me, and I don't live in an area that's particularly welcoming to people like me.

Despite these challenges, I find joy in playing dress-up when I'm by myself. It makes me feel cute and happy, even though I have to keep my clothes and accessories hidden. If you met me in public, you would have no idea about this side of me.

I started this blog to share my thoughts and feelings, as well as the outfits I find cute and inspiring. This is a safe space for me to express who I am and connect with others who might be going through similar experiences.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy following along on my journey!
